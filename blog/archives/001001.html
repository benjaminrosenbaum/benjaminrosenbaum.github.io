Address Bar Alphabet

Here's a little meme-game for you.

Presuming you have a browser, like Google Chrome, with auto-completion in its address bar, try typing the 26 letters of the alphabet, and report its top suggestion for each. This gives you a little window into your surfing habits. I expect a lot of these will be common across most people, but some will diverge.

You can tell I've been looking up flights to the US recently. It's also interesting what a stranglehold Google has on my online world...
