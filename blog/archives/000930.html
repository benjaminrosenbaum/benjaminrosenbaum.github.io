My Name is LUCA

My name is LUCA
I live in the early sea
I think you've seen me before, yeah,
In fact I think you're me

If you've got something really keen
Some kind of protein, some kind of gene
Just don't keep it to yourself
Just don't keep it to yourself
Just don't keep it to yourself...

I can make some proteins.
I can't make them very well.
But sharing makes a heaven here
where we could have had a hell.

These proteins' structures will carry on
Even after the code is gone
They're the footprints that I leave
They're the footprints that I leave
They're the footprints that I leave...
