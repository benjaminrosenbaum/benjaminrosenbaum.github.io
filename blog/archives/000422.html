Two for two for two

Hmm, well, this is on the web, so I guess it's okay to announce...

Sean Wallace has posted the confirmed contributions to Prime Books' entry into the crowded melee of Year's Best Anthologies, and it seems that editor Rich Horton, too, has chosen both "A Siege of Cranes" and "The House Beyond Your Sky", for his fantasy and SF best-of's, respectively.

Whee again!
