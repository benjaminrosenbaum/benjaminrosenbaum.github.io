Magnet Poetry from Stacy's Coffee Parlor, #2

A continuing series.


meager lizard !
your tiny cozy mouth
on a mud drip night
and a plump poet stagger ing
in lucky galoshes
through rain

come hold my wet hair
we will manage

skin the red moon
