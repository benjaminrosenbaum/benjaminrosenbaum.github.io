"Imaginary Book Club" Panel at Wiscon

The able Julia Rios has managed to upload the videos we took of the "Imaginary Book Club" panel at Wiscon 2011. Thrill to see the panelists discuss:

  • John Joseph Adams's fabric-oriented genre anthology "Material Velour, Color Unknown" and Oscar Wilde's lost cyberpunk novel
  • the steamy garden romantica classic, "Trailer Gnome"
  • Elizabeth Bear's dragons-in-Iowa novel, "Beneath Yellow Skies"
  • Nelson Algren's protocyberpunk Chicago sf/noir, "The Arm With the Golden Man"
