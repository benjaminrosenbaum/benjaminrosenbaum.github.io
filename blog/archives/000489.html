Hidenori Watanave Illustration for "The House Beyond Your Sky"

"The House Beyond Your Sky" is apparently being translated by Yoshio Kobayashi and illustrated by Hidenori Watanave for Hayakawa SF, Japan's premier SF magazine. I haven't actually heard from the publisher yet, but both of them wrote me to tell me they liked the story!

Here is Hidenori's awesome illustration. He was the illustrator who did the Other Cities illustrations in Hayakawa a while back.

Updated: Ha! Like, the instant before I posted this I got the email from Hayakawa SF forwarded from Susan. So it's official.
