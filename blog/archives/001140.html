Sunday, November 13, 2016

A(r)gented

I'm thrilled to announce that I'm now represented by literary agent John Silbersack, of Trident Media Group. His impressive list includes a few friends of mine, like John Kessel and James Patrick Kelly, and he had really smart things to say about The Unraveling, a novel I have been working on for a long time. He asked me about my plans and I said "basically I'm going to write a lot of weird books that don't necessarily go together" and he said "that will make my life difficult but interesting."

(Apologies for the multilingual pun in the title of this post).
