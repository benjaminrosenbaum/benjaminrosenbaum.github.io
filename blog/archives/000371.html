A Song By Noah, who is Two and a Half

I float away the next morning, I float away the magnet

I float away when I wake up
and then I check the time

It even says four of two...

And the time is pepperoni,
pepperoni,
pepperoni yoyo,
yoyo, Aviva's yoyo --
yoyo, Aviva's yoyo
