Argh! I am a Scheduling Ninny

I happened to glance at my airline ticket. The one which is taking me to Worldcon.

It cheerfully indicated that I am arriving at 9 pm on Thursday.

"Oh, ticket," I said. "Oh, ticket, this is not right. This will not do at all! For I have a Panel at Six P.M.!!"

The ticket looked sympathetic, yet unyielding.

Argh!
