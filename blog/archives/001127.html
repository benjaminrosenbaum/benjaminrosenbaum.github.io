Wiscon Schedule 2016

Wiscon Ahoy!

Friday
Group, Individual, Hive Mind
4:005:15 pm
Conference 1
You think it's obvious where you leave off and everyone else begins? Maybe it's not so obvious, not among human cultures, and certainly not in biology. What is an individual? What is a group? How does the identity of "individual" and "group" vary? After exploring variations in earthly organization, we'll tackle SF where alien characters originate from different types of social or biological organization. Who creates characters that truly reflect those differences? Who can't quite shake human-centered or dominant-culture-centered myopia?
Annalee Newitz, Grá Linnaea, Benjamin Rosenbaum, Isabel Schechter, R. Elena Tabachnick
Saturday
BAD DECISIONS
2:30-3:45 pm
Michaelangelo's
Ill-advised teen romance. Getting apprenticed to an evil wizard. Shady doings in Riverside. Dislocated portal-fantasy refugees or frumm zombie hunters. Or maybe something else altogether. How did we get here? Our feeling of dread is barely matched by exhilaration. Come witness our folly.
Ellen Kushner, David Moles, Benjamin Rosenbaum, Delia Sherman
Sunday
How Does One Write Near Future SF?
4:005:15 pm
University C
We are looking at global warming, resource wars, the migration of large numbers of people, a fragile world economic system and a world political system that seems unable to deal seriously with real problems. How does a writer extrapolate from this mess into the future?
Amanda Bankier, Eleanor A. Arnason, Eugene Fischer, Pat Murphy, Benjamin Rosenbaum
